Data Visualisation II - Indian Premier League Statistics

Indian Premier League (IPL) is a twenty20 cricket that takes place in India
every year by eight teams where each represent an Indian city. Eleven seasons
of IPL has been contested from 2008 to 2019 and all data collected from these
seasons are utilised to demonstrate the different statistics with unique idioms.

IPL Team's Season Wins

This is a heat map representing each teams total wins for all years the team had contested in. The white boxes mean that team had not participated in that certain year or season.

IPL Team's Home and Away Win Percentage

This is a stacked bar chart demonstrating the total team's win percentage (%) for both home and away matches.

IPL Player's Country of Origin

This is a proportional symbol chart illustrating the total number IPL players country of origin on a world map

Top Ten IPL Batting Leaders

This visualisation shows the top ten all time batting leaders with the most runs made in their entire IPL career.

All IPL Players Batting Hand Style

The donut chart displays the proportion between total number of right hand and left hand players for all seasons.
